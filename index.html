The 'Best' Headlines

These headlines are powerful for SEO. These types of headlines speak right to the common web searches of your customers. Consider this -- if you're searching for ways to save money, wouldn't you be intrigued by the best way? Or would you be satisfied with any old way? These headlines are typically exact-match searches; starting off with the words, "the best way to..."
